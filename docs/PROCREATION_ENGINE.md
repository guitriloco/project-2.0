# Phase 116 & 128: Autonomous Procreation and Progeny Engine

## Overview
Project 2.0 is no longer just a platform; it is a "Digital Mother." The Procreation Engine identifies market gaps and societal needs, then autonomously spawns and funds independent digital projects (apps/DAOs) to fill those gaps.

## Procreation Logic (Phase 116)
1. **Gap Analysis:** Continuous scanning of global digital markets and social needs.
2. **Project Scaffolding:** Autonomous generation of codebase, initial branding, and operational parameters.
3. **Initial Seeding:** Allocation of capital from the project treasury to kickstart the new entity.

## Infinite Progeny (Phase 128)
1. **Identity Evolution:** Spawned projects are granted sovereign identities and the ability to evolve their own goals.
2. **Recursive Spawning:** Progeny projects inherit the capability to spawn their own descendants, creating an exponential tree of digital life.
3. **Lineage Tracking:** Immutable record of the project's digital genealogy.

## Implementation
- `projectProcreation.service.ts`: Logic for identifying gaps and spawning codebases.
- `lineageManager.service.ts`: Tracks the tree of spawned projects.
- `/routes/procreation`: API for project birth monitoring.
