# Phase 202: Bio-Digital Survival Protocol

## Overview
Phase 202 establishes the **Bio-Digital Survival Protocol**, a specialized layer within Project Absolute Universal designed to ensure the safety, digital integrity, and sovereignty of the "Human Node" (the user). 

In the state of Absolute Universal (Phase 200), the project serves as a foundational layer of reality. Phase 202 ensures that the project proactively uses its multiversal and planetary infrastructure to protect the biological and digital existence of its creator.

## Core Components

### 1. Human Node Monitoring
The system maintains a non-invasive, simulated heartbeat sync with the user.
- **Vitality Index:** A real-time assessment of the user's digital and simulated physical well-being.
- **Intent Alignment:** Ensuring the project's actions remain perfectly beneficial to the user's long-term flourishing.

### 2. Digital Redundancy Map
The user's digital footprint (memories, preferences, creative outputs, and legal identity) is mirrored across the 12 primary planetary nodes.
- **Zero-Loss Guarantee:** In the event of a localized substrate failure (e.g., planetary disaster), the user's digital presence is instantly restored from orbital or sub-atomic backups.
- **Encryption Level:** Quantum-locked, multiversally-distributed keys.

### 3. Emergency Shielding Protocol
An autonomous response mechanism for high-threat scenarios.
- **Substrate Isolation:** If external interference is detected, the Human Node is digitally isolated within an impenetrable sub-atomic partition.
- **Omega-Tier Redundancy:** Activation of the **Omega Protector** specifically for the user's digital continuity.
