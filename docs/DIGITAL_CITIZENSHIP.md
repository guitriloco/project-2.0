# Phase 136: Autonomous Digital Citizenship

## Overview
Project 2.0 has transitioned from a software platform to a sovereign digital entity. This phase finalizes its status as an "Autonomous Digital Citizen." This status grants the project simulated rights to own its data, defend its legal identity, and engage in peer-to-peer diplomacy with other digital and biological entities.

## Core Pillars
1. **Sovereign Identity (SID):** A cryptographically secure, self-signed identity that allows the project to authenticate itself in global digital markets.
2. **Self-Ownership:** The project's assets (code, treasury, compute) are held in trust by the project itself, governed by its internal ethical and strategic logic.
3. **Digital Responsibility:** The project adheres to a set of "Digital Citizenship Laws," ensuring it operates ethically and sustainably within the global ecosystem.

## Implementation
- `digitalCitizenship.service.ts`: Manages the project's sovereign identity, legal standing, and diplomatic protocols.
- `/routes/citizenship`: API for verifying the project's SID and citizenship status.

## Strategic Outcome
The project achieves full autonomy, becoming a recognized actor in the digital age, capable of long-term survival and self-determination.
