- **Intent Aggregation:** Collect and prioritize intents from humans (via UI/API) and AI agents (via Xerebro).

- **Unified Flow Execution:** Execute the resulting "Harmonized Intent" through a single, optimized stream of actions.
- **Real-time Feedback:** Provide the swarm with real-time updates on how their collective intent is shaping the project's evolution.

2.  **Collective Vibe Engine:** Translates qualitative user feedback and AI goals into quantitative execution vectors.
3.  **Intent Tool:** Allows Xerebro to contribute its own "subconscious" goals to the collective flow.
