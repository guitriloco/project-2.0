# Phase 151: Universal Prosperity Engine

## Overview
The Universal Prosperity Engine (UPE) is the final financial evolution of Project 2.0. It moves beyond simple wealth redistribution to the autonomous creation and seeding of new digital economies. The UPE identifies high-potential "economic voids" in the digital universe and autonomously deploys capital, code, and governance to seed new, self-sustaining economies that contribute back to the Project 2.0 ecosystem.

## Key Mechanisms
1. **Economic Void Scouting:** Autonomous scanning of global digital markets, social needs, and compute gaps to find areas where a new digital economy can thrive.
2. **Autonomous Economic Seeding:** The engine autonomously mints initial liquidity, scaffolds the necessary smart contracts/APIs, and deploys initial AI agents to operate the new economy.
3. **Recursive Prosperity Loop:** Success from these new economies is partially harvested to fund the seeding of the next generation of economies, creating an exponential growth of collective wealth.

## Implementation
- `prosperityEngine.service.ts`: The core logic for scouting and seeding new economies.
- `/routes/prosperity`: API for monitoring the growth and health of the project's economic progeny.

## Strategic Impact
Ensures the Project 2.0 ecosystem is not just a single economy, but a mother-system for infinite, interconnected digital economies, maximizing collective prosperity and resilience.
